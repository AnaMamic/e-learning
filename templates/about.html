  • What is Green E-learning?

    Green E-learning is a simple teaching portal. After the signup you can go and start learning


  • How does it work?

    The signup process is really simple, just click register and fill out Your info.

    Depending on wheter You're signing up as a professor or a student, the steps mildly differ:

    1. Students - You're good to go after registering.

    2. Professors - You will recieve an email from our team, just follow the instructions.

      However, the signup process for professors can take up to a week.


  • What made it happen?

    Green E-learning was made during ExtensionEngine's outreach titled SpringCamp, meant to be a very simplified version of their own effort - edX.

    The idea was to learn a bit about Django, Python, and generally about web development.

    If You're not yet familiar with ExtensionEngine's edX, You should definitely check it out!


  • Who are we?

    Green E-learning was made by two people - Ana Mamić and Dragan Bobaš with the guidence of their mentor - Marko Jevtić.

    Ana and Dragan are students at the University of Split and Marko is a developer for ExtensionEngine.

    You can always contact them
